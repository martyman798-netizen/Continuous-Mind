1. BASIC GRAMMAR RULES
---------------------
Rule 1: Sentences start with a capital letter and end with . ? !
Example: I eat apples. Do you like bananas?

Rule 2: Nouns - person, place, thing. Singular/plural: cat/cats, man/men.
Example: The dog runs. The dogs run fast.

Rule 3: Verbs - action words. Present: walk/walks. Past: walked.
Example: She walks to school. She walked yesterday.

Rule 4: Articles: a/an (indefinite), the (definite).
Example: A cat. An apple. The sun shines.

Rule 5: Pronouns: I, you, he, she, it, we, they.
Example: I am happy. He is tall. They are friends.

Rule 6: Adjectives describe nouns. Big house, red car.
Example: The small bird flies high.

Rule 7: Adverbs modify verbs/adjectives. Quickly run, very happy.
Example: She sings beautifully.

Rule 8: Prepositions: in, on, at, to, from.
Example: In the house. On the table. At 5 PM.

Rule 9: Tenses - Present Simple: I eat. Present Continuous: I am eating.
Past Simple: I ate. Future: I will eat.
Example: I eat breakfast daily. I am eating now. I ate lunch. I will eat dinner.

Rule 10: Questions: Who? What? Where? When? Why? How?
Example: What is your name? Where do you live?

--------------------------------------------------------------
Subject-Verb: I run. You jump. He sleeps. She eats. It flies. We play. They dance.
Subject-Verb-Object: I eat apple. You read book. He kicks ball. She drinks water.
Negative: I do not run. You are not happy. He does not eat.
Questions: Do you run? Is she happy? Where is the cat?

More sentences:
The sun rises in the east.
Birds fly in the sky.
Children play in the park.
I go to school every day.
She works in an office.
We eat dinner at night.
He drives a red car.
They live in a big house.
Water is wet. Fire is hot.
Books are interesting.

4. DIALOGUES FOR CONVERSATIONAL LEARNING
---------------------------------------
Dialogue 1: Greeting
A: Hello! How are you?
B: I am good. And you?
A: I am fine, thank you.
B: What is your name?
A: My name is John.
B: Nice to meet you, John.
